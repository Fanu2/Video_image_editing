About This Repository

This repository contains Python scripts that allow you to edit and manipulate videos and images. Each script focuses on a specific task, such as adding borders, resizing images, applying filters, and creating animations. Below is a brief explanation of each script in this collection.

Scripts

  • 7_circles.py: Generates a colorful visual animation with rotating circles, perfect for visual projects or video backgrounds.
  • Check_requirements.py: Verifies if all necessary libraries and dependencies are installed for smooth functioning of the video/image editing scripts.
  • Love_poem_1.py: A script to generate a video with text overlay of a romantic poem, set to a serene background.
  • add_audio.py: Add an audio track to a video file, supporting various formats like MP3, WAV, and AAC.
  • add_border_imagemagick.py: Adds a border to an image using ImageMagick, ideal for framing photos or thumbnails.
  • add_caption_to_video.py: Adds subtitles or captions to a video. Ideal for creating informative or accessible video content.
  • add_fade_in_out.py: Adds fade-in and fade-out effects to video clips to create smooth transitions between scenes.
  • add_logo_to_video.py: Watermarks your video with a custom logo, ensuring your brand or copyright remains visible.
  • add_music_to_video.py: Integrates background music into your video, with options to loop and synchronize with the videoâ€™s length.
  • adjust_brightness.py: Adjusts the brightness of videos or images, giving you control over visual clarity and aesthetic.
